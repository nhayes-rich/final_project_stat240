Spending by Recipient

The following plot shows the most common recipients of spendings by spending amount, count, and average spending amount. The results can be filtered by candidate and year.

Observations

In 2016, Cliinton, Trump, and other candidates all spent the most on political marketing companies like GMMB, Old Towne Media Inc, and Giles-Parscale. This is the case for the candidates in 2020 as well.
Similarly, in both 2016 and 2020, candidates had the highest average spending in media companies.
In terms of the number of spendings to a particular recipient/company, companies related to travel received the most number of payments.

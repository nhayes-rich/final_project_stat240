Spending by Purpose

The following plot shows the most common spending purposes by spending amount, count, and average spending amount. The results can be filtered by candidate and year.

Observations

In 2016, Cliinton, Trump, and other candidates all spent the most on media. This is also true for Biden, Trump, and other candidates in 2020.
Similarly, in both 2016 and 2020, candidates had the highest average spending in media.
In terms of the number of payments, for both 2016 and 2020, candidates most frequently paid for payroll and travel.
